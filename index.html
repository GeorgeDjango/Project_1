About me


Hi there! My name is George Django Parker, and I am a Worcester based music tutor specialising in Bass, Guitar, Drums and Ukulele.

Growing up in Spain, I started a band with my close friends and began gigging most weekends and became a working musician at the age of 16, picking up many useful skills along the way. At age 20 I moved back to the UK and studied music at universtiy and joined a new originals alternative rock band, solidifying my music theory and studio skills.

After working in various bands for a number of years I decided to begin teaching music as a private music tutor and a peripatetic music tutor working in various schools and music teaching academies across Worcestershire.

Projects


Underground Ocean

"Underground Ocean" were an original alternative rock band based in Worcester that gigged across the UK.

Consisting of Stuart MacDonnell on rhythym guitar and vocals, Matt Beddoes on lead guitar, George Parker on bass and backing vocals and Lee Evans on drums, the band released an EP and three singles "Get Me Out", "I See Through You" and "Hide You".




The Mayor's Nest · Released Music

The Mayor's Nest

"The Mayor's Nest" is a chilled out indie duo based in Fernhill Heath, Worcester and gig solely in living rooms, to no audience.

Consisting of George Parker on guitar, keys, drums and Rebecca Parker on guitar ukulele and vocals, the husband and wife duo self-produce their music and so far has released a handful of covers including "Wicked Game" by Chris Isaak, "Ain't No Sunshine" by Bill Withers, "Everybody Wants to Rule the World" by Tears for Freas, etc.




django.

"django." is a lo-fi garage-rock / indie-rock project based in Worcester and will prospectively gig in the future

Written and produced by George Parker, "django." is a solo project which will involve Harvey Jones on bass guitar and Sebastian Baldwin on drums during live performances, so far, there has only been a demo EP, and a couple of singles released, but there is an album in the works which will be released eventually.
